Engineering and Design

Our engineers and designers are the backbone of AppDev. Designers are responsible for creating the look and feel of apps, and engineers write the code to make them a reality. Taking on a role in engineering and design is a great way to get practical experience in the app development industry.

Engineering and Design are high skill positions, meaning you will need to complete the appropriate training program or demonstrate proficiency in the technologies covered therein. We offer four training programs.

  1. iOS Engineering. Learn to make iPhone and iPad apps.
  2. Android Engineering. Learn to make Android apps.
  3. Web Services Engineering. Learn to make web APIs.
  4. Design. Learn to design quality user interfaces and experiences.

You can attend more than one training program. Training starts at the beginning of each semester, at which point we will reach out on multiple channels. To ensure that you are notified, you can contact us and indicate your interest in joining or ask any questions you may have.

Finance and Business

There is no formal training program for finance and business. Prior experience is preferred, but not required as our current business leadership can mentor you in the skills you will need. Please contact us if you are interested.

Community Outreach

There is no formal training program for community outreach. Some experience within AppDev is preferred, but not required as our current leadership can mentor you in the skills you will need. Please contact us if you are interested.
